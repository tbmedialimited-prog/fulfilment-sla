v7-backfill
Built: 2026-05-25
Changes from v6:
  - NEW: /api/backfill endpoint for loading historical orders (30 days back)
    * Paginates through Mintsoft statuses 4, 5, 6
    * Resumable: returns "next_url" if it ran out of time before finishing
    * Capped to BACKFILL_DAYS=30 days back (configurable via env var)
  - Status discovery now scans 1-30 instead of 1-10
  - Daily cron unchanged (it just catches yesterday's new orders)

USAGE:
  1. Visit /api/backfill
  2. If response has "done": false, click next_url
  3. Repeat until "done": true
  4. Each call processes ~50 seconds; you may need 5-15 calls for 30 days of data
