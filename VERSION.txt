KEY DISCOVERY (with credit to user):
  Real working DPD tracking URL format:
  https://track.dpdlocal.co.uk/parcels/<tracking>*<5-digit-suffix>
  
  Example: 15976912925627*21327 for postcode GL103WB
  The 21327 is NOT derived from the postcode - it's stored somewhere
  (most likely returned by Mintsoft on order detail).
