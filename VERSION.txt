v6-multi-status
Built: 2026-05-25
Changes from v5:
  - Mintsoft sync now pulls statuses 4, 5, 6 (Dispatched + Invoiced + Completed)
    instead of just status 4. This catches orders that moved past "Dispatched"
    after their dispatch day.
  - Added /api/discover-statuses to identify which OrderStatusId values exist
    in your Mintsoft account. Visit it once to confirm the right IDs.
  - MINTSOFT_DISPATCHED_STATUSES env var lets you override (default "4,5,6").

After deploying:
  1. Visit /api/discover-statuses to see which statuses have data
  2. Visit /api/cron to trigger a fresh sync (will pull all dispatched + invoiced + completed)
  3. Watch order count grow significantly past 133
